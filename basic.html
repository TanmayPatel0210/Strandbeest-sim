This is a visual of Jansen's Linkage. It is the fundamental building block of the Strand Beest. Unlike wheels or modern robotic limbs, this mechnism mimics the movement of animals' Limbs by purely Mechanical parts. The simulation runs on some basic math formulas to constraint the ponints and the rod lengths are defined in such manner that it mimics a limb of a walking being, those rod lengths are known as "Holy Numbers".
